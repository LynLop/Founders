ABOUT US

At Founders, we believe that life’s simplest pleasures are often the most meaningful—a warm, handcrafted donut paired with a perfectly blended coffee. Our café was born from a deep love of baking and a heartfelt desire to create something beautiful, comforting, and delicious—something that satisfies the cravings of every guest who walks through our doors. Each of our donuts is made fresh daily in small batches, using time-honored techniques and thoughtfully sourced ingredients. From delicate glazes to inventive seasonal flavors, every bite is crafted to delight the senses and brighten your day. We’re passionate about creating a space that feels cozy and welcoming—whether you're stopping in for a quick morning treat, catching up with friends, or simply enjoying a quiet moment with your favorite latte. We don’t just make donuts—we make moments worth savoring.
